About Me


My name is John Adam Taylor, and I am from Westchester, NY, born on April 14, 2003. I just turned 21! Growing up, I was always fascinated by how things worked, which blossomed into my passion for computer science, specifically AI. This curiosity began in my childhood, inspired by movies like Toy Story (Toy Story 2 is my favorite), where inanimate objects came to life and interacted with humans. These films sparked my imagination, making me wonder what it would be like if my toys could understand and respond to me. Over time, this curiosity evolved into a deeper interest in understanding the magic-like qualities of technology, particularly AI.

In addition to my love for coding, I enjoy playing various sports such as basketball, golf, tennis, and volleyball. Physicaly activity is a form of meditation for me and I enjoy staying active and having fun with friends and family. Traveling is another passion of mine; one of my favorite places in the world is Florence, Italy. I believe traveling is one of the best experiences life offers, and I am incredibly grateful for the opportunities I've had to explore different cultures and places.

Currently, I am pursuing my studies at Binghamton University in Upstate NY with a major in Computer Science focusing in Artifical Intellegience, where I continue to nurture my interests and passions. Below is a place to message me with any questions, I am open to work!
